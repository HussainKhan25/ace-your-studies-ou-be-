📋 Game Rules & Instructions

🎯 Objective:

  • Navigate through the maze to reach the golden star (★)
  • Answer 5 educational questions correctly to unlock the goal
  • Complete levels within the time limit
  • Avoid traps and collect power-ups

🎮 Controls:

  • Desktop: Arrow keys or WASD
  • Mobile: Touch the directional buttons
  • Swipe: Swipe in any direction to move

🔍 Game Elements:

  • 👨‍💻 Player: Your avatar (glowing green)
  • ❓ Questions: Educational challenges (pink/red)
  • ★ Goal: Level completion target (gold)
  • 💀 Traps: Lose a life if stepped on (red)
  • ⚡ Power-ups: Special bonuses (blue/gold)
  • █ Walls: Impassable barriers (gray)

📚 Question Topics:

  • Digital & Basic Electronics
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Computer Networks & Systems
  • Machine Learning & AI
  • Cybersecurity & Software Engineering
  • Mathematics & Finance

💎 Scoring System:

  • Correct Answer: +200 points
  • Time Bonus: +10 points per second remaining
  • Power-up Collection: +150 points
  • Level Completion: +500 points
  • Perfect Round: +1000 bonus points

⚠️ Challenge Features:

  • Lives System: You have 3 lives per game
  • Time Pressure: Each level has a countdown timer
  • Question Timer: 30 seconds to answer each question
  • Progressive Difficulty: Harder questions at higher levels
  • Trap Penalties: Lose a life when hitting traps
